AUTHENTIC INDIAN CUISINE

Family and Flavor

At Taj, the Guntaka family brings a rich blend of tradition, passion, and authentic Indian cuisine to South Portland. Sourcing spices directly from India the chefs craft every dish from scratch. They've built a reputation for unforgettable flavors. Hemalatha, the heart of the kitchen, prepares each meal with care, while Sai and Sudarsana ensure every guest feels like family. Whether you're enjoying a meal in their warm, inviting dining room or sipping on a handcrafted cocktail at their new bar, Taj promises a dining experience that's both flavorful and heartwarming. Book a reservation today and taste the love and dedication that goes into every dish!
